Our Spaces

Design Portfolio

From entryways to living rooms, kitchens to bedrooms - we create spaces that inspire.

01

Entryway Designs

The entryway sets the tone for the entire home, combining elegance with everyday function. Inspired by classic European foyers and minimalist traditions, our designs feature statement lighting, refined details, and curated accents to create a warm, inviting first impression.

02

Living Room

The living room is the heart of the home, a space where comfort meets style and personal expression shines. Our design concepts balance elegance with livability, drawing inspiration from contemporary aesthetics, timeless European charm, and natural textures.

03

Kitchen

Mica: Durable and budget-friendly, mica finishes offer a wide range of textures and colors, ideal for everyday use in high-traffic kitchens.
Acrylic: Known for its glossy, mirror-like finish, acrylic adds a sleek, modern touch while being easy to clean and maintain.
Glass: Glass panels bring elegance and depth, reflecting light to make kitchens feel spacious while enhancing a contemporary aesthetic.
04

Bedroom Essentials

A bedroom should be a sanctuary that blends relaxation with personal style. Our design concepts focus on creating tranquil, cozy spaces through thoughtful use of color, lighting, and textures.
